⚠ Food Service Decline — Context

Food Service revenue fell from £48,405 to £27,939 — a decline of £20,466 (-42%). This is largely driven by rotational listings such as Eurostar and Bloomberg, which typically place orders over 2–3 month windows, 1–2 times per year.

These accounts are expected to reorder in due course. Meanwhile, our new GLL Better listing across 65 leisure centres is set to grow this channel meaningfully, adding consistent recurring volume from cafes, vending, and party bags.
